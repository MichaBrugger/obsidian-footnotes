# A3: popup editor on vs off

Fixture marker to navigate from: jump me[^1] now.

[^1]: the detail to land on

With `Edit footnotes in a popup` ON:

- [ ] Inserting opens the popup at the caret; the caret never leaves the text
- [ ] Pressing the hotkey inside `[^1]` opens the popup on its detail
- [ ] Hotkey again / Escape / click outside closes it

With `Edit footnotes in a popup` OFF:

- [ ] Inserting jumps the caret to the new detail at the bottom
- [ ] Pressing inside `[^1]` jumps to the detail, centered; pressing on the detail jumps back
